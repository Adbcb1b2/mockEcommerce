FAQs


Do you offer international shipping?

Sadly not at the moment, we ship to the UK only but this is something we will be looking into for the future.

Are there minimum order quantites?

No, we don't have a minimum order. You can order as little or as much as you like.

Can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your package.

How can I order more than one of each item?

You can either click the 'Add to Basket' button multiple times or you can go to your basket and do it from there.
